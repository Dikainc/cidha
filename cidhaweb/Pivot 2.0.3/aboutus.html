About us Learn what we do

Community Centre for Integrated Development and Good Health Awareness (CIDHA) is a non-governmental, not-for-profit making organization committed to reducing poverty and improve the health of Nigerian citizens and promote respect for ethics and human rights in health care policies and practices especially for vulnerable and marginalized groups such as People Living with HIV/AIDS, MARPs, Women, Out of School Youths and persons in hard to reach Communities. CIDHA is registered with Nigeria Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC).

CIDAH VISION what we stand for

To see a developed Nigeria where extreme poverty is eradicated and quality health care services is available, affordable and accessible to all citizens irrespective of sex, sexual orientation, age and status or disease condition.

CIDAH MISSION Our Road map!

To work with different partners and stakeholders to reduce poverty and improve the health of Nigerian citizens and promote respect for ethics and human rights in health care policies and practices especially for vulnerable and marginalized groups such as People Living with HIV/AIDS, MARPs, Women, Out of School Youths and persons in hard to reach Communities.

Aim and Objectives Our Road map!

  • Promotion of small scale and medium enterprise.
  • Promotion of cooperative farming in our society.
    • Empowering and showcasing farming product across the world.
  • Promotion of agricultural and agro allied business in our society.
  • Promotion of farming and sales of rice for local consumption and export.

Our deiscoveries Stats

CIDHA has identified some challenges facing our farmers today in Nigeria, they include

  • Inadequate skills and education
  • Inadequate finance in loans and grant for the farmers
  • Inadequate farm tools and equipment for the farming
  • Inadequate food supply in our homes

CIDHA Project International is actively pioneering a unique solution to agricultural changes in the world This approach will engage massive participation in farming and agro-allied businesses.
