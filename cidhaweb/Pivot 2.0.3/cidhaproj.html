About CIDHA Project what we do

With the rate of unemployment and hunger in Nigeria, CIDHA is using rice as a major commodity in our local homes and market to pioneer a better way or solution to unemployment, agricultural, food and financial problems and challenges in our society. This implies that if many Nigerians are empowered to go into rice farming, the production and sale of rice will increase thereby providing more than enough food for consumption and also we will have more to export because production of rice is higher than consumption thereby creating employment for many.

METHODOLOGY our methods

CIDHA adopts the PT&E approach, which stands for:

  • Participation
  • Training
  • Empowerment

Participation our requirements

Each member is to participate by opening a cooperative account with a token of $35 (N12,000) and become an associate member of CIDHA project International.

Training get proactive

CIDHA will provide the members with proper training and orientation they need and also provide them with the necessary skills, workshop and the financial need as a farmer and for their day to day activities.

Empowerment more than skill acquisition

Each member will be empowered economically to be financially free and self employed through rice farming and selling. A member will become a cooperative team leader by referring only 3 persons who will also refer their own 3 persons each making it a total of 12 members under him/her and a total of 13 members in each cooperative group. The team leader will see to it that other members in his/her group also grows to become team leaders. By this, they are already on their way to attaining financial freedom.

Once this obligation is meant by an individual you will naturally become a great and a potential farmer and thereby you will always smile to the bank all the time. Note that our platform is designed to provide financial support for rice farming and other agricultural farming for the cooperative members through creation of a support system. This is the time to support our Government through agricultural value chain factories that will naturally boost the economy of our great nation.
